Shuku Sha - How to Play - v1.0

Uh oh, you fell in salt. The goal of the game is to shoot all of the incoming Berts before they reach you. If you don't they take you back to their lair and crown you king. Different types of Berts have different health values, and will give different amounts of gold on death. Be careful though, shooting a wall will make you lose a coin!

As of v1.0, there are two types of Bert:
Common Bert: 1 HP - 1 Gold
Lovestruck Bert: 3 HP - 2 Gold

Movement:
Turn Left: A
Turn Right: D
Fire: Spacebar

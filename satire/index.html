Shuku Sha - How to Play - v1.1

Uh oh, you fell in salt. The goal of the game is to shoot all of the incoming Berts before they reach you. If you don't they take you back to their lair and crown you king. Different types of Berts have different health values, and will give different amounts of gems on death. These gems can be spent on upgrades.
You get some gold each time you clear a wave, and you also get three gold for clearing a wave without shooting a wall.

As of v1.1, there are four types of Bert:
Common Bert: 1 HP - 1 Gem
Lovestruck Bert: 3 HP - 2 Gems
Smug Bert: 5 HP - 3 Gems
??? Bert: ?? HP - ?? Gems

Movement:
Turn Left: A
Turn Right: D
Fire: Spacebar

Upgrades:
Upgrades increase linearly in cost by 25 gems and price is shared between both types.
Firing Speed: Makes you able to shoot faster by 50ms.
Bullet Damage: Increases the damage of the bullet by 1 HP, and adds 75ms to firing speed.

Stages:
There are currently two stages. Beating the first will unlock the second, and beating the second will unlock a new skin gamble tower.
